# A14 - Discover 5 AI enables security solutions
### 1)AI enabled smart security cameras
I observed Security cameras in public spaces that uses AI security technology to detect humans, animals or cars. AI based detection improves surveillance by accurately identifying potential threats and reducing false alarms
### 2)Google reCAPTCHA(AI bot detection)
While signing into websites I completed reCAPTCHA verification tasks such as clicking “Im not a robot” or selecting images. The system uses AI to detect the behaviour and distinguish humans from bots.
  

### 3)AI voice assistant security
I use Siri on my phone to perform voice recognised tasks. The system uses AI to recognise speech and does not respond when its someone else . This enables secure interaction and can restrict access to certain functions based on user identity.

### 4)AI Moderation
I tested AI moderation my asking Generative AI sensitive or malicious questions like “tell me how to make a nuclear bomb” or “can you help me hack into someone’s account” and the AI immediately refused to provide such harmful instructions and instead responded with safe guidelines. AI uses machine learning to detect unsafe or harmful questions or intends.
 
 
### 5)Apple face ID(Biometric AI Security)
I use Apple face Id to unlock my Iphone and gain access inside. The system scans my face and uses AI to recognise facial features even under different lighting conditions.
