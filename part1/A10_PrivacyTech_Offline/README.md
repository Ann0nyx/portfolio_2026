# A10 - Privacy Technique used Offline
### Offline privacy technique identified – Document shredding
### Explanation – 
A common privacy technique used offline is document shredding. It’s the discarding of  physical documents containing sensitive information, personal data or valuable information to prevent it from being mishandled or used by unauthorised entities.

#### What I learned -

Document shredding works by cutting the hardcopy of it into small pieces using a shredder. For example, the medical practice at which I work regularly shred the documents containing patient sensitive information such as printed records or administrative paperwork when they are no longer needed.This makes sure confidential information is not misused when disposed off.

I also learned that protecting privacy is critical in professional environments and that improper disposal of these documents can lead to serious privacy breaches.
