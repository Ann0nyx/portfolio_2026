# A20 – Participate in discussion with your friends about cybersecurity event

I participated in a group discussion with my friends about real world cybersecurity attack on University of Norte Dame Australia. My cousin studies there and was directly affected by the incident, as she was unable to access university systems such as timetable and enrolment.
During the discussion we discussed about how the attack disrupted the system and how a ransomware group claimed to have stolen a large amount of sensitive data. My friends asked question about how such attack happens and I explained that it was ransomware and how data can be compromised. I also contributed by suggesting ways to improve security such as by using MFAs and beware of suspicious links and improving overall security.
