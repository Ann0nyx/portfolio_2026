# A3 – Security Concepts at Home

## Security concepts Identified -

### 1)Confidentiality – Password and Biometric door lock
In my home, confidentiality can be seen maintained by biometric door locks that require a code or fingerprint of a registered family member to gain access to the house. This ensures that only authorised individuals can enter the house preventing strangers or buglers to gain access in. These systems hence maintain confidentiality by protecting physical assets and sensitive information stored within the house.

### 2)Integrity – Home surveillance cameras
Integrity at my home is maintained by installing security cameras around the house. These cameras continuously monitor activity and help prevent unauthorised tampering with property or belongings outside or inside the house. Furthermore the camera acts as a deterrent, as individuals are less likely to alter, damage or interfere with things if they know they are being recorded. This also provide reliable evidence incase of incidents, allowing homeowner to verify what actually happened.

### 3)Availability – Home Wifi network and Internet access
The wifi router provides internet connectivity to multiple devices within the household, allowing home members to access communication platforms, educational resources and entertainment systems whenever needed. For Instance, I use the home Wi-Fi for daily access to LMS and student connect for learning, research and  submitting assignments making it essential for my daily life. The router is typically operated to function continuously 24/7 ensuring authorised users can access the network at any time without interruptions. This demonstrates availability as systems and services remain accessible when needed.
