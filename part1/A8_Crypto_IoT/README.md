# A8 – Cryptography in Internet of Things devices

### Cryptography Identified in Internet of Things devices – Secure Screen Mirroring(Airplay)

### Explanation –
One such cryptographic use in Internet of Things is the secure screen mirroring technologies such as apple airplay. This allows a user to wirelessly connect their mobile device to smart TVs or other smart devices to share audio and video content. These connections use encryptions to ensure the data is transmitted securely and that no outside unauthorised entity can modify or access it.

#### What I learned – 
Airplay works by establishing a wireless connection with the devices over secured wifi network. Apple airplay uses AES encryption for both video and audio streaming to ensure that the content is protected.For example, I connected my iphone to the smart tv using airplay to mirror my screen. The device required confirmation or a pairing code before streaming. After confirmation, and on connection, the phone screen is displayed on the TV, displaying secure communication through devices.
