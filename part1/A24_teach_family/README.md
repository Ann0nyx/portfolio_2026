# A24 – Teach your family about cybersecurity topic of your choice

### Topic taught – Phishing awareness

I taught my sister about phishing, as to how cyber criminals use it to steal personal information such as passwords, bank details and OTPs. I explained as below that phishing attacks often involve messages that pretend to be from banks or delivery services.

I gave her real world examples and also taught her how to identify phishing attempts by checking for signs such as suspicious links, urgency in messages and unusual sender email address.I additionally warned her not to click random links and share her OTPs with anyone.

Finally, I advised her to use strong passwords and enable Two-factor identification for better security.
